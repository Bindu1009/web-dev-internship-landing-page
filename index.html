About the Internship Program

Ready to turn your coding knowledge into practical skills? Our 6-month Web Developer Internship at Cognifyz Technologies offers a hands-on experience in building modern web applications. You'll work alongside our experienced team, contribute to live projects, and get valuable insights into the software development lifecycle.

This full-time program is designed to help you grow from a student to a confident web developer. High-performing interns may even be considered for full-time roles after completing the program!

Why Intern with Us?

Hands-On Experience

Work on actual client projects, not just theoretical exercises.

Dedicated Mentorship

Build Your Network

Connect with professionals and other passionate interns in the tech community.

What We're Looking For

We're seeking enthusiastic individuals with a passion for web development. Ideally, you have:

  • Solid understanding of **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.**
  • Basic knowledge of Git version control.
  • Strong problem-solving abilities and a willingness to learn.
  • Good communication and teamwork skills.
  • Currently enrolled in or a recent graduate of a Computer Science or related program.
